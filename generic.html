Experience

Hello! I’m Evelise, but feel free to call me Eve. I'm a recent graduate from the University of Houston - Downtown, where I completed my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. My ambition is to build a career as a software engineer, with particular interests in mobile development, fintech, and gaming, working across both backend and frontend technologies.

Previously, I worked as a Production Engineering Trainee at Tesla, where I contributed to lowering inventory holding costs by developing an inventory tracking dashboard, reducing troubleshooting time with Python scripts, and improving performance analysis for production lines. This role honed my skills in Python, MySQL, and configuration management, as well as my ability to implement efficient solutions in a high-stakes environment.

In my free time, I love building new projects that challenge me and teach me new tools, which I believe will be invaluable in my future career. Whether it’s developing a cryptocurrency tracking app or a mental well-being application, I am always eager to explore and innovate.

I speak both Portuguese and English, so if you want to practice Portuguese, feel free to reach out! :)
